# Portfolio

### About me :wave:
Hi, my name is Ali. Since 2024, I have been studying, practicing, and working to become a QA professional. Coming from a strong UI/UX and technical background, I finally found a field where I can grow, think analytically, and make a real impact.In this portfolio, I want to share my knowledge of quality assurance and highlight the practical skills I’ve gained through real projects, self-study, and hands-on work. I’m committed to growing in this field, sharpening my testing mindset, and raising the standard of every product I touch.

### My experience :office:
I am currently working as a QA Engineer at Atria Solutions, focusing on system testing, defect reporting, and ensuring reliable user flows.
Previously, I worked at HerronTech, where I combined UI/UX design and QA, contributing to user-centered interfaces while validating functionality and usability.
This combined experience allows me to deliver high-quality products with a strong focus on both usability and reliability.
